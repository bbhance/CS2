Available commands:
 - "help" = This! :D
 - "north" = move north
 - "south" = move south
 - "east" = move east
 - "west" = move west
 - "up" = move up
 - "down" = move down
 - "get" item = pick up item and put in inventory
 - "drop" item = drop item in the current room
 - "inventory" = look at items in your inventory
 - "exit" = end game
 
Items to pick up:
 - Weapons; Hand Gun, Shot Gun, Sniper-Rifle, Bat
 - Consumables; Med-kit, Water, Gum
 - Misc.; Books
